FAQs

Is Dhananjaya Bandara Open on Sundays and public holidays? +
Yes, we are open on Sundays and public holidays.
How can I make a tentative Reservation? +
We can do a tentative reservation over the phone. It will only be valid for one week. Within this week, you have to conform the reservation by making an advance payment.
I made a tentative reservation; how can I book the same date again? +
You can contact us again to re-confirm your preferred date.
Can I get the Quotation over the phone? +
Yes, we can provide quotations over the phone.
Do you have a wedding package? +
Yes, we offer various wedding packages tailored to your requirements.
Do I have to come to your Studio to get a dress? +
While we recommend visiting the studio for fittings, we can arrange consultations remotely.
Do you charge for the appointments? +
We do have a nominal charge for appointments which will be credited towards your final booking.
Will Dhananjaya come for the Dressing for the Going away? +
Yes, Dhananjaya will personally oversee the dressing for your going away event.
Do I need to pay transport for Going Away dressing? +
Transport charges may apply depending on the location.
